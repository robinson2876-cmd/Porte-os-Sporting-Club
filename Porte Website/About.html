About the Club

Sports in the Urban Parish of Puerto Bolívar, in the Canton of Machala, especially football, has long been a space for community athletes, where the best football in El Oro province was played and great professionals emerged to shine in Ecuadorian football. However, due to social challenges and the neglect of local and provincial authorities, our children and youth are increasingly exposed to social vices such as crime, drug use, micro-trafficking, and violence. Through this project, we are working to rebuild our nation—rescuing young people and showcasing to the world the incredible athletic talent in our community. These young athletes are raw diamonds, ready to be refined through faith, values, education, and sport.

Physical education, and football in particular, has evolved over the years in many ways. However, it has often ignored one of the most important elements: spirituality, and the ethical and moral principles that support a good life. Today, academic preparation must go hand-in-hand with athletic training to develop more humane, humble athletes who lead by example. Our mission is to promote better teaching and learning processes in football, so that the sport is practiced with both passion and intellect. We aim to nurture players who truly stand out and bring pride to their roots through real, meaningful competition.

This approach turns football into a unique pilot project—different from the rest. It requires strong psychological discipline, agility, strength, endurance, and immense willpower. We strive to instill knowledge and purpose in our players, helping them become athletes who can write their own stories. Football is known as the king of sports, one of the most celebrated disciplines worldwide. Today, it is practiced by everyone—men and women alike—without discrimination. At Porteños Sporting Club, we believe in football not just as a game, but as a powerful tool for life transformation.
